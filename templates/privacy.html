Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PixelBoost AI. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you share with us while using our image enhancement service. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Information We Collect

When you use PixelBoost AI, we collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Images you upload for processing
  • Basic usage data such as upscale scale selected
  • Standard server logs including IP address and browser type

We do not collect your name, email, or any personal account information unless you contact us directly.

3. How We Use Your Images

Your uploaded images are used solely for the purpose of AI enhancement:

  • Images are temporarily stored on our server for processing
  • Processed output images are uploaded to Cloudinary for delivery
  • 2x and 4x output images are uploaded to Cloudinary for fast delivery and deleted from local server immediately after
  • 8x and 16x output images are served directly from our server for maximum quality and deleted automatically after download
  • We do not use your images for training AI models
  • We do not share your images with third parties

4. Cloudinary Storage

PixelBoost AI uses Cloudinary as a cloud storage provider to deliver your enhanced images. Images stored on Cloudinary are retained temporarily and may be deleted periodically to manage storage. Cloudinary's own privacy policy applies to data stored on their platform. You can review it at cloudinary.com/privacy.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • All connections are secured via HTTPS
  • API credentials are stored securely in environment variables
  • Local files are deleted immediately after Cloudinary upload
  • No user accounts or passwords are stored

6. Cookies

PixelBoost AI does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. Basic session functionality may use minimal browser storage to improve your experience.

7. Third Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate PixelBoost AI:

  • Cloudinary — Image storage and delivery
  • Render.com — Application hosting

These services have their own privacy policies which govern their use of data.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal accounts or identifiable data, there is nothing to request deletion of beyond your uploaded images. If you have concerns about an image you uploaded, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of PixelBoost AI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at moinarif309@gmail.com or visit our Contact page.
