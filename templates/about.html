What is PixelBoost AI?

PixelBoost AI is a free, powerful image upscaling tool that uses cutting-edge artificial intelligence to enhance your images. Whether you want to upscale a photo for printing, sharpen a blurry image, or enhance AI-generated artwork — PixelBoost AI delivers professional-grade results in minutes.

🧠

How Does It Work?

PixelBoost AI uses Real-ESRGAN — a state-of-the-art deep learning model trained on millions of real-world images. Unlike traditional resizing which simply stretches pixels, our AI predicts and generates new pixels based on learned patterns, producing sharp, natural-looking results even at extreme zoom levels.
